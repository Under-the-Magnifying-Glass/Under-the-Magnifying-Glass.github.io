Sherlock Holmes

First introduced to the world in 1887 by Arthur Conan Doyle, this idiosyncratic detective is featured in 4 novels, 56 short stories and many screen adaptations.

Hercules Poirot

A brilliant mind created by the famous Agatha Christie, appearing in 33 novels, two plays, and more than 50 short stories published between 1920 and 1975.

Nancy Drew

Young, intelligent and talented, she is one of the most popular US detective figures, starring in 175 novels written by a series of authors in the years 1930 to 2003.
